# Tiny Jam — Support

## Tiny Jam: Pocket Music Toys

A growing collection of pocket-sized music toys — a groovebox, a synth, a drum machine — that fit in your pocket and start making sound the second you open them. Free on iPhone and iPad, with a few optional in-app purchases. No ads, no tracking.

# Frequently asked

**How do I unlock an instrument?**
Tap a locked rack, then "unlock" — it's a one-time in-app purchase. Some instruments are free to play without any purchase at all.

**I bought a rack (or the Master Pass) and it's not showing up.**
Tap **Restore Purchases** in the app — that re-checks your Apple account and re-enables anything you've already bought. If it still doesn't show up, email me at [nikkikoole@gmail.com](mailto:nikkikoole@gmail.com?subject=Tiny%20Jam%20restore%20purchase) and I'll sort it out.

**What can I buy?**

- **Acid Rack** ($2.99) — unlocks the acid rack (two 303s, a 909, an 808).
- **E-Piano** ($2.99) — unlocks a warm electric-piano keybed.
- **Master Pass** ($5.00) — unlocks every rack, now and future.

**Is my data collected?**
No. No ads, no tracking, no accounts.
