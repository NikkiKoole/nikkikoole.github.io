story time

Did you dream about toys coming to life when not looking?

I know I did. I had my little figurines in my school desk, pretending they were living there in a little room. Once, Miss Coby asked me all about it.

Anyway, now I am all grown up, and I don't dream about that anymore unless it's in a dream. It doesn't happen when I'm not looking (I think). It takes a whole lot of effort to make toys come to life.

Mipo Puppet Maker by Mipolai

Mipolai, Nikki Koole

Mipolai, mipolai? What kind of name is that even?

When I was about 4 I had a friend called Nikolai, and my name is Nikki, so together we were Nikki & Nikolai. Except I didn't pronounce it like that, nor register it like that. He was Mipolai, and I was Nikki — pronounced as Nitti.

And when I was thinking about what to call this little studio, I remembered this, and how Mipolai was the best name ever, according to myself at 4 years old.
